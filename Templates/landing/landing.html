How It Works

Follow these simple steps to integrate with Zoho and generate tokens

1

Create Zoho Client

Register a client in the Zoho API Console to get your Client ID & Secret.

Tip: Add http://localhost:8000/Templates/connector/oauth_callback.html as an authorized URI.
2

Enter Credentials

Provide your Client ID, Client Secret, region, and desired scopes.

3

Authenticate

Initiate the OAuth flow – Zoho handles secure authorization.

4

Retrieve Tokens

Receive your access and refresh tokens securely.

Tip: Save your refresh token for future renewals.
